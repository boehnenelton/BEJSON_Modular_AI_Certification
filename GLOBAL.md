# BEJSON Global Standard (Level 1)
**Status:** Authoritative | **Nomenclature:** Boehnen Elton JSON
**Handling Certification:** LEVEL 1 (FOUNDATIONAL)

## 📋 Level 1 Competencies
Level 1 Certification verifies mastery of the basic fundamentals of the BEJSON ecosystem:
1. **Metadata Mandatory Keys:** Absolute knowledge of `Format`, `Format_Version`, and `Format_Creator` (Elton Boehnen).
2. **Matrix Architecture:** Understanding the tabular separation of `Fields` and `Values`.
3. **Positional Integrity:** The strict requirement that every value must align perfectly with the index of its corresponding field.
4. **Nomenclature:** Authoritative use of "Boehnen Elton JSON".

## 🏗 Core Principles
- **Positional Integrity:** This is the most critical rule. A record is invalid if its values do not exactly match the field schema by position.
- **Type Safety:** Adherence to defined types (string, integer, boolean, etc.) within the matrix.

## 🎯 Qualification Mandate
You are **UNQUALIFIED** for any Level 2 or Level 3 modifications unless you have mastered these Level 1 basic fundamentals. Unauthorized modifications without foundational certification are prohibited.
